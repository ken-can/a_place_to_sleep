Nickelodeon's HALO Award

Hosted and executive produced by Nick Cannon, Nickelodeon HALO Awards is an annual awards show that recognizes real-life kids that are doing extraordinary things in their communities and “Helping and Leading Others” (HALO).

In addition to being awarded a grant for their organization and scholarship funds, each HALO Award honoree is given an once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to meet and connect with celebrities that share their same passion for service.

In 2015, Jessica was one of the first to win the HALO Effect Award! This was the first time that Nickelodeon had given this award. The Effect Award became a monthly award, as opposed to a yearly award. Her charity was given $10,000!

"You can always help someone, no matter how little you have." --Jessica Collins
