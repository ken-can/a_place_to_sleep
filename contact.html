    Children are recommended by the Family Resource & Youth Service Center Coordinators at each school. A Place to Sleep has helped children from every shcool in the Shelby County School System, as well as, the immediate surrounding counties.

    Some children too young to be enrolled in school have been recommended by Head Start, the Salvation Army, ALC Pregnancy Center, and Seven County Services. On occasion, referrals come from other agencies such as churches, Operation Care, and The Serenity Center. These famileis are still required to work with the Family Resource/Youth Service Centers for an officeial referral. This insures that the children are enrolled and attending school, and enables the schools to help the family find other needed resources.
